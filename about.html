About My Life

Hey I’m Parker! I’m an 18 year old photographer & entrepreneur based in Cincinnati, Ohio. Creative work and photography has always been a passion of mine. My goal is to develop my skills and build a strong portfolio while also connecting with other people and artists to create, collaborate, and continue growing my portfolio.

Outside of photography, I’m deeply interested in entrepreneurship and the impact creative work can have when it’s paired with business. Over the past few years I’ve built small ventures, taken on freelance projects, and learned how to market, communicate, and create value through my skills. From photoshoots to web development, I approach every project with intention and a focus on growth.

I also design and build websites, including this one. Web development has taught me valuable problem-solving skills and has opened up a community of like-minded, driven individuals. Whether I'm doing a photoshoot or coding a website, my goal is always the same: Develop my skills, connect and collaborate with others, and build a strong entrepreneurial foundation.
