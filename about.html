About My Life

Hey I’m Parker! I’m a 18 year old photographer & entrepreneur based in Cincinnati, Ohio. Creative work and photography has always been a passion of mine. My goal is to connect with other people and artists to create, collaborate, and continue growing my portfolio.

Over the past few years I’ve put a lot of time into building small businesses. Having little luck with many different ideas, Jones Curb Design grew to be more successful than the others. I first started it a few summers ago and have been building it up ever since, finding new ways to improve each design.
